Top Destinations

  • Paris, France – The City of Love, home to the Eiffel Tower and world-class art.
  • Rome, Italy – Ancient ruins, the Colosseum, and authentic Italian culture.
  • Maldives – Crystal-clear waters, coral reefs, and romantic beach resorts.
  • Bali, Indonesia – Tropical paradise with rice terraces, temples, and beaches.
  • New York, USA – The city that never sleeps, with Broadway, Times Square, and Central Park.
  • Swiss Alps, Switzerland – Snowy mountains, skiing, and breathtaking scenery.
